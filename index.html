Welcome to the Majji Research Group

Our mission is to advance technologies that drive society toward a sustainable future by improving access to energy, advanced materials, and clean water. We combine colloidal science, multiphase flow, and electrochemical engineering principles to investigate fundamental processes, address critical challenges in industry and the environment, and develop innovative solutions for applications in electrochemical energy storage, electrochemical synthesis, water treatment, oil and gas, and carbon dioxide transport. We leverage both experimental and modeling approaches to achieve these goals.
